As of now vanced has dropped support and it will break in 2-3 years.
To have similar experience to vanced, you can use youtube on firefox browser android.

How to have almost same experience?

  1. Go to play store and download Firefox Nightly

  2. Enable debug mode from settings

  3. If you have Mozilla account you can make collection of your extention right away, if not Sign up

  4. After signing up, create your own collection and add these extenstions:
    1. uBlock Origin
    2. SponsorBlock
    3. TamperMonkey for dislike counter, install the script into tampermonkey from here

  5. Once you have saved your collection, give it a good name(necessary thing to do)

  6. Now note down your user-id from the address bar(8 digits)
  7. As you have turned on debug mode(from step 2) open up setting scroll to advanced section & click "Custom add-on Collection".
    • Paste your id & your collection name in next box
    • Click OK and browser will restart to apply these changes...

  8. Once browser is restarted goto the "Add-on" section and install all the extenstion...
    • Dont forget to install Return Youtube Dislike script from step 4.c

  9. Now You are good to go!! Sign-in with your google account and enjoy the ad-free experience
    • PiP Mode


    • SponsorBlock + Return Youtube Dislike...

    10. You can also add your desired extenstion by just adding those in your collection and just repeat step 7 by just clicking on ok Sometimes Firefox dosent refresh extenstions thats why...
